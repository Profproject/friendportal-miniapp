How FriendPortal works

FriendPortal is a referral-based earning platform built on the TON blockchain.

How you earn:

  • Invite users using your referral link
  • When they activate access — you earn TON
  • Rewards are credited instantly

Referral rewards:

  • Level 1 — 0.5 TON
  • Level 2 — 0.25 TON

Advertising packages:

  • 25 TON — broadcast message
  • 75 TON — top button (7 days)
  • 250 TON — premium placement (30 days)
